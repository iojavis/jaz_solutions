Who we are.

Est 2025

For the ease of investors...

Jaziel Solutions Investment Consultants is a top-tier advisory firm in Tanzania, linking global investors with high-impact opportunities in key sectors such as tourism, mining, housing, and manufacturing. We simplify the investment process through regulatory support, distributor services, and strategic market entry. Our focus on sustainability and inclusive growth aligns with Tanzania’s Vision 2025 and the UN SDGs.

  • Client-Centricity: Tailored solutions prioritizing investor needs and goals.
  • Efficiency: Faster turnaround times through streamlined processes and technology.
  • Integrity: Transparent, ethical practices fostering trust and long-term partnerships.
  • Sustainability: Projects aligned with UNSDGs to promote environmental and social responsibility.
  • Innovation: Cutting-edge strategies to navigate Tanzania’s dynamic market.

Our mission

"We empower investors by streamlining processes, reducing barriers, and offering end-to-end support—from opportunity identification to project execution. Our commitment to efficiency, transparency, and sustainability ensures every investment delivers lasting value."


Our Vision

"To be Tanzania’s premier gateway for local and global investments, driving sustainable development for the benefit of communities worldwide."

Our expertise spans high-impact sectors such as community-based tourism, mining, real estate, and manufacturing. As a dynamic business enterprise, we not only facilitate investment but also drive inclusive and sustainable economic growth through innovative solutions and strategic partnerships.

Central to our operations is a full-service investment facilitation model—covering regulatory compliance, licensing, and approvals—complemented by robust distributor services that ensure seamless delivery of products and services across diverse markets.

We develop enterprising market entry strategies grounded in local knowledge and collaborative networks, enabling smooth integration into key sectors. From feasibility studies to full-scale execution, our project and distribution management ensures timely delivery, regulatory adherence, and operational efficiency.
